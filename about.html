About Purrfectly

Hi, we're Luiza and Tyler, the creators of Purrfectly – and devoted cat parents to three amazing cats. If you're anything like us, you've probably found yourselves lost in the endless aisles of pet food, deciphering ingredient lists, and trying to give your beloved cats the very best without emptying your wallet.

Juggling work, life, and the well-being of multiple cats, we were constantly frustrated. It felt like a never-ending quest to identify the truly healthy foods, dodge questionable ingredients, and ensure our cats received balanced nutrition – all while keeping an eye on the budget. It shouldn't be this hard to do right by our furry family members!

Let's be honest, a lot of cat food out there just doesn't cut it. And if you're trying to provide variety (because who wants to eat the same thing every single day?), navigating the market to find multiple *good* options can feel overwhelming. We knew we wanted to give our cats the vibrant, healthy lives they deserve.

That's why we built Purrfectly. We desperately needed a simpler way. We envisioned a single, go-to resource where we could quickly scan products, instantly compare ingredients, keep track of what each of our unique cats loves and thrives on, and confidently plan meals that were both nourishing and budget-friendly.

Purrfectly is the very tool we rely on daily to ensure our cats get optimal care without financial stress. Every feature is born from real-life experience, designed by cat parents who truly get the daily challenges and aspirations we share: providing top-notch care without it becoming a stressful, time-consuming, or bank-breaking endeavor.
